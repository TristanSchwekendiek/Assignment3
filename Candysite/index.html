The Halloween Staple

Welcome to efrutti, where we specialize in crafting high-quality candy products. Our signature gummi burger is a testament to our commitment to excellence. While it may look like a regular burger, we assure you that it is made entirely of premium-quality gummi candy. Each bite offers a burst of fruity flavor that is sure to delight your taste buds. Our gummi burger is perfect for special events, gift baskets, or as a fun treat for any occasion.

Our Mission

At Efrutti, we understand that managing food allergies and sensitivities can be a challenging task, especially when it comes to finding delicious treats that meet your dietary restrictions. That's why we're dedicated to providing every customer with the opportunity to savor our premium gummi candies without compromising on taste and texture. We collaborate with local authorities and the most rigorous third-party laboratories to develop policies and procedures that guarantee our ingredients and production methods cater to the needs of modern families. This ensures that you can relish our candies with complete peace of mind.
